Sailing

One of the great pleasures of the Côte d’Azur is sitting at a beachside café with a nice, cool drink in your hand watching yachts glide across the shimmering surface of the sea. Occasionally, I dream of joining them out there in the fresh breeze and spray. But then I remember my student days, when I tried my hand at sailing. No, let them get cold and wet. I’ll stay here on dry land with my drink. . .
